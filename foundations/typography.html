Typography is critical to our users success. We’ve carefully choosen a hierarchy and type scale that gives clarity and also has enough density to feel like a developer tool, not a website.

Typefaces

We utilize Inter to ensure a Retool feel when users move between platforms.

font-family: "Inter var", Inter, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, system-ui, Segoe UI, Roboto, Helvetica Neue, Ubuntu, sans-serif;

Headings

Using a consist and logical hierarchy ensures that elements in the UI are clear and easily recognizable when scanning the page. Text sizes, styles and layouts were selected to balance content and UI and to foster familiarity.
